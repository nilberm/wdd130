Website Purpose

The Dev Mota Portfolios And Landing Page website was designed for those people who want to put their name, product, brand, company, or anything else on the internet. Today the world is increasingly digital, no one uses paper maps to find an address anymore, they search the internet and there they get a guide to get to their destination. My goal is to do the same for you, make it possible for your customers to find you and know how they can get in touch with you because you will have your place on the internet, appearing in the first responses to surveys of websites in your field, making you stay easier to capture new customers and make your business grow.

What we do

We build websites that are well designed to appear in the first places in search results, that are fast to load on both your computer and mobile phones, and that follow all the accessibility standards so that your customers have the maximum comfort and ease using your portfolio and for you to have a high conversion rate from your customers to your product or service.

What you can have

Along with your portfolio or landing page you can choose whether you want to add up to 3 more pages, 1 for contact, where we can have an email box where your customer can write the message and it goes directly to your email with your customer's information, the second page can be a FAQ, so you can dedicate a page just to answer the most frequent questions asked by your customers, and the third page we can develop a page about a specific product so that you can give more information about a certain product or subject to your customer.

My experience

I've been making websites for over 2 years, creating high quality websites and keeping the design fidelity, with responsive designs for all screens on any device. We have ready-made website templates and you can choose the one that most relates to your business, or if you prefer, we can also create an exclusive design for you, just contact us for a quote. We analyze your business, target audience, customer characteristics, goals with the site, so we can meet all your needs and reach your target audience.

Our plans

Closing our premium package you will be entitled to a home page and up to 3 more pages (FAQ, Contact and Specific Product) without any additional value, you will also receive a free website domain for 1 year and receive a discount on the monthly payment for website hosting and maintenance for possible bugs and minor changes. In the common package, you are entitled to the home page, be it a portfolio or landing page and the website domain free for 1 year, the additional pages have an increase in the value of the package.

Questions

For more information or questions you can contact us, we will always be available to provide our services and answer your questions as soon as possible so that we can place your place on the internet.
